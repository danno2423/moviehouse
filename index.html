Wondrous Memories of Things Past

About

Rick Spector has worked with mature adults since 1976, but he's ever a kid. Rick recalls seeing his first movie, Cinderella, at the Tyson theater in 1958 at age 7. "Wow, I thought the screen was a big TV. I couldn't understand how the picture was projected from a booth behind me." "It's part of human nature. People love to reminisce. Looking back to days gone by helps us to feel better about ourselves. Moviehouse Productions helps folks to unlock those precious memories."
